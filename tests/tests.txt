# Tests
## Test 1 - Authentication lock protects CRM access [sanity]
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies signed-out and synthetic non-member users cannot access or modify the CRM while local recovery remains available; provisioned-member RBAC is verified manually with the real account.
Steps:
1. Open the app signed out and confirm only the secure login screen is shown
2. Click Google ile Giriş using the available synthetic test account
3. Confirm a non-provisioned account receives the active-authorization warning and cannot enter the CRM
4. Confirm local recovery remains available and the synthetic account cannot bypass the membership gate
Expected: Signed-out and synthetic non-member identities remain blocked without a blank-screen crash; an authorized real member proceeds to the CRM, where Hedef Planlama opens confirmed-registration details including candidate/offer source, shows visually consistent branch/date/advisor/education filters that combine correctly, separates collection metrics, warns about missing paid-course data, and prevents advisor allocations from exceeding the branch course target.
## Test 2 - Card and note installments require explicit selection
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies card and note plans require an explicit installment choice and preserve it in the offer.
Steps:
1. Sign in as the provisioned founder, open Kampanyalar ve Fiyatlar and confirm the recovered QNB Finansbank · Card Finans tariff appears in the active central list
2. Create an offer for an İzmit candidate, select Kredi Kartı and an active tariff; confirm saving is blocked until a Taksit Sayısı is selected
3. Select a non-maximum card installment, add a Senet alternative with its own plan and installment count, save, refresh, then click Aç and Revize
Expected: No maximum installment is auto-selected; main and alternative choices calculate independently, persist after refresh, and are restored in Revize without overwriting the source.
## Test 3 - Guest offer and alternatives
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies a signed-in member can prepare a proposal for an unregistered name with at most two alternatives.
Steps:
1. Open Yeni Teklif, type a unique name and valid unique phone, select a source without selecting a registered candidate, then choose education and a campaign
2. Confirm one main offer is shown, add two alternatives and save the offer
3. Confirm the row is marked Misafir with two alternatives and that Adaya Dönüştür is available to a verified member
Expected: The guest offer appears with phone, source and Misafir record type; no fourth card can be added; verified-member candidate conversion remains available for manual RBAC validation.

## Test 4 - Offer copy, revision chain and lifecycle
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies grouped main/alternative visibility, lifecycle actions, protected deletion, and coordinated candidate-offer registration persistence.
Steps:
1. Open a saved offer and click Kopyala; save it
2. Confirm the copy editor opens as an independent offer without a parent revision reference; save persistence is verified manually with an authorized member
3. Revise the original, save the revision, and confirm the original remains unchanged
4. Share the revision, open the candidate's Teklifler tab, and inspect the grouped main and alternative choices plus version history
5. Try deleting an offer that has a newer revision, then delete the newest revision and confirm the destructive action
Expected: One offer group shows every alternative separately; latest valid offers expose Kesin Kayda Dönüştür; completed records expose Kayıt Detayı; candidate and offer update together after refresh, stale concurrent registration attempts are rejected and refreshed, and protected deletion rules remain intact.

## Test 5 - Offer Center filters are isolated
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies offer filters do not change candidate-page filters.
Steps:
1. In Adaylar choose a status and date filter
2. In Teklif Merkezi filter by source, record type, phone and Tüm Tarihler
3. Reset only the Offer Center filters, then return to Adaylar
Expected: Phone formats +90, 0-prefixed and digits-only find the same record; source uses sourceId/source data; the five compact KPI cards use only latest offer versions, react to active filters, filter the list and expose clear info popovers; Offer Center shows İndirim Türü instead of Teklif No while offer-number search remains available; the shared dark filter bar keeps primary filters visible, expands secondary filters and resets without changing candidate filters.

## Test 6 - Duplicate phone protection and candidate editing
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies duplicate candidates are blocked and a newly created candidate remains visible under the active date filter.
Steps:
1. Note an existing candidate phone, owner and first offer date
2. Try the same phone with spaces, +90 and digits-only formats in New Candidate, New Offer and Adaya Dönüştür
3. Confirm each action is blocked and the warning identifies the candidate, consultant and prior date
4. Open that candidate, edit a non-key field and save; then create a unique candidate and keep the Bu Ay filter active
Expected: No duplicate is created; edits preserve linked dates; the unique new candidate appears immediately in Bu Ay and remains visible after refresh.
## Test 7 - Central pricing data survives refresh
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies pricing data remains stable after edits and refreshes across browser sessions.
Steps:
1. Create a unique Saat Bazlı education type and campaign with list rate 3000, cash rate 1500 and 20 default hours
2. Verify İndirim shows %50, save the campaign and refresh the page
3. Reopen and save the campaign, add an inactive card tariff, confirm Arşiv opens automatically, then return to the app from another browser session
Expected: Unit rates remain 3000 and 1500, discount remains %50, totals do not multiply, inactive tariffs appear in Arşiv, and a second authenticated device sees the same central pricing data after returning to the tab.

## Test 8 - Archive and restore preserve identity and history
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies authorized archive actions are recoverable and do not physically delete linked CRM history.
Steps:
1. Sign in as founder, create a uniquely named candidate and save one offer for that candidate
2. Archive the offer and candidate, then confirm neither appears in active lists, KPIs or reports
3. Open Arşiv, restore both records and return to the candidate detail
4. Confirm the original candidate ID, offer, notes, tasks, shares and revision history are still linked
Expected: Archive behaves like removal from active work without physical deletion; restore returns the same records and relationships, and duplicate phone protection remains active while archived.

## Test 9 - Branch and role boundaries remain isolated
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies founder, manager and advisor permissions do not expose or overwrite records outside their scope.
Steps:
1. Sign in with an İzmit manager account and confirm only İzmit archive and branch records are visible
2. Sign in with an İzmit advisor account and confirm only assigned records are visible and Arşiv management is unavailable
3. Attempt to modify a Körfez record or protected user-management collection from either scoped account
4. Sign in as founder and confirm both branches plus Kullanıcı Yönetimi remain available
Expected: Managers stay inside their branch, advisors stay inside their assigned records, unauthorized writes are rejected, and out-of-scope records remain unchanged.

## Test 10 - Reports, targets and calculations remain unchanged
Viewport: mobile
Description: Verifies v90 infrastructure protections do not alter approved report filters, target cards, buttons or offer math.
Steps:
1. Open Dashboard, Adaylar, Teklif Merkezi and Hedef Planlama at mobile viewport and inspect the approved controls
2. Apply branch, date, advisor, education and status filters, then compare visible counts and totals with their filtered rows
3. Create cash, card and note calculations using the same campaign, including a non-maximum installment and two alternatives
4. Refresh and reopen the offer, reports and target details
Expected: Approved buttons and layouts remain usable; filters stay isolated; candidate, offer, ciro, tahsilat and target totals use the same records; payment totals, installments, down payment, finance difference and discount authority remain unchanged after refresh.
